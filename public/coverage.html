UCP Spec Coverage (unofficial, live)

Every normative MUST in each pinned UCP spec version is accounted as a kill-rate-validated check, a documented exemption, or an open gap — no silent omissions. Each requirement links to the exact line of the pinned official spec. This page is generated from the same data the CI gate enforces: if it were stale or coverage regressed, the build would be red. Covered requirements are also browsable as the check register, graded per the rubric.

Methodology. Requirements registers are extracted from the pinned official spec and quote-verified verbatim by a CI gate on each push. A check only counts when it clean-passes AND kills 100% of its injected defects on a known-good reference (the official Flower Shop sample or our oracle-validated controlled fixture). Exemptions require a written justification and are machine-checked against double-booking. The coverage CI gate fails the build if this page's data goes stale or accounted coverage ever decreases (ratchet). Roadmap →
